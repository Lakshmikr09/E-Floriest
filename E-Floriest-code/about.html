About Us

Welcome to E-Florist, an innovative initiative designed to improve the flower market with a technologically advanced digital platform. E-Florist endeavors to emerge as the premier digital marketplace for flower trading, catalyzing growth and resilience across the global floral supply chain.

Our Mission

E-Florist aims to create an innovative ecosystem connecting growers, wholesalers, and flower shop owners to increase efficiency, sustainability, and transparency. This platform allows growers to monitor the market and adjust based on demand.
